About Us

PSHS-SMC is the first regional campus of the PSHS System, a specialized public high school in the Philippines. It caters to scientifically and mathematically gifted high school students from all over the country especially those in Mindanao. It is located in Barangay Sto. Niño, Tugbok District, Mintal, Davao City.
